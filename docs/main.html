I am currently a PhD student in mathematics at GSSI. The problem I currently work on is a problem in kinetic theory, which is to rigorously pass from the Schrödinger equation to a linear Boltzmann type equation in the weak coupling limit.

I am broadly interested in problems that lie at the intersection of dispersive PDEs and probability.

Prior to this, I was in a joint Master's program on mathematical modeling at the University of Hamburg and the University of L'Aquila. During my master's I was a scientific assistant in the CFEL-CMI group at DESY, using tools from machine learning to solve problems in molecular dynamics. My thesis was on the approximation of the solution to the HJB equation, for smooth, nonlinear, finite-horizon optimal control problems.
