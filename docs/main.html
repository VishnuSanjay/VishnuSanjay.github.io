I'm a mathematician, currently Wallis professor of mathematics at the University of Oxford. Previously I held positions in Bonn, Paris and Pisa. I work in the field of stochastic analysis. My interests lie at the intersection of analysis, probability and mathematical physics. I like to think about the random functions which appear in many physical theories, including quantum mechanics and quantum field theory.
